Solid Blocks. Lasting Cool.

MURN ICE CUBES & BLOCKS FACTORY is situated in Tshiombo outside Thohoyandou, Limpopo. This is a factory built to cater for the Venda nation — ensuring their beverages are always cold.

Why Choose MURN ICE CUBES?

  • We aim to empower the youth of Venda and surrounding areas to grow and succeed.
  • We offer part-time opportunities at our factory for community development.

What We Do in Our Company

We produce dependable ice cubes & blocks for both individuals and businesses. We also engage in motivational speaking and youth support — because not everyone gets a chance to hear real stories and guidance.

Our mission is to deliver fresh ice quickly, reliably, and with excellent customer service. With the launch of our website, ordering ice has never been easier.
